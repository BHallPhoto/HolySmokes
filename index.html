About Us

Holy Smokes Smoke Shop in Foresthill opened it's doors May 1st 2018 with one goal in mind. Providing Foresthill, Michigan Bluff, and Todds Valley with all of the wonderfully fun new Smoking accessories, such as Glass, Vape & CBD. And still keeping it classic with Cigars, Cigarettes and Tobacco Products. Don't forget about all the fun treasures and gifts. Shirts and Sweatshirts geared special for Foresthill and surrounding areas, and of course all of the amazing artwork our local artists have displayed here in our Smoke Shop in hopes to sell. There is a little something for everyone, and please allow our knowledgeable & kind staff help you find or order what you are looking for.
